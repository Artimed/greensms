How it works

Three steps to a transfer

1

Receive SMS

Your bank sends a notification. GreenSMS reads your last messages and instantly parses the bank name, amount, balance and card number.

2

Scan or enter recipient

Scan a recipient's QR code or enter a phone number / account number manually. The app builds the correct transfer command for your bank automatically.

3

Send via SMS or USSD

Confirm and send — either directly via SMS (e.g. Sberbank 900) or via USSD dial (GTBank, FirstBank, SBI and others). No app, no internet required.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

📲

Smart SMS Parser

Reads last 10 bank SMS, extracts amount, balance, card last-4, operation type (incoming / outgoing / transfer) and reference number.

📷

QR Transfer

Generate your personal QR code to receive. Scan a recipient's QR and the transfer command is formed instantly — just confirm and send.

⌨️

Transfer Without QR

No QR? Enter a phone or account number directly. The app looks up the correct bank command format and opens the SMS or dialer for you.

💰

Daily Limit Tracker

Set your daily transfer limit. The app tracks every sent transfer and shows remaining balance at a glance on the home screen.

🌍

12 Languages

Interface available in English, Russian, Hindi, Armenian, Kazakh, Uzbek, Filipino, Indonesian, Vietnamese, Urdu, Bengali and more.

🔒

100% Offline & Private

No internet permission. No accounts. No analytics. All data stays on your device in a local SQLite database. Always.

Installation

How to install

1
Download the APK
Use the Download button above. The file is about 73 MB.
2
Allow unknown sources
Go to Android Settings → Security → allow installs from your browser or file manager.
3
Open and install
Tap the downloaded file and follow the prompts. Tap Install.
4
Grant permissions
Allow SMS and Camera permissions when the app asks. These are required for reading SMS and scanning QR codes.
