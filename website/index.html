What it does

Everything local, nothing leaves your phone

📲

SMS Parsing

Automatically reads your last bank SMS messages and extracts amount, balance, card last-4 and operation type.

📷

QR Transfers

Generate your personal QR code. Scan a recipient's QR and instantly form the correct SMS or USSD transfer command.

🔌

USSD & SMS

Supports both SMS-based (Sberbank 900) and USSD-based transfers (GTBank, FirstBank, SBI and more).

🌍

Multi-country

Works with banks in Russia, Nigeria, India, Armenia and more. 12 interface languages included.

🔒

Fully Offline

No internet permission. No accounts. No data leaves your device — ever. SQLite storage only.

💸

Daily Limit

Built-in daily transfer limit tracker. Set your own limit and see remaining balance at a glance.

Installation

How to install

1
Download the APK using the button above.
2
Allow installs from unknown sources in Android Settings → Security.
3
Open the downloaded APK and tap Install. Grant SMS and Camera permissions when asked.
